About Us

Who We Are

In the heart of Helsinki, on a lively street corner, stands Wasabi, a Japanese restaurant that draws locals and tourists alike with its authentic flavors and welcoming atmosphere. The restaurant is the brainchild of Yukio and Hana, a couple who left their lives in Kyoto five years ago to chase a shared dream. With a vision of bringing the warmth and richness of Japanese cuisine to Finland, they poured their savings into transforming a small, dimly lit space into a vibrant haven. Inspired by Kyoto's timeless charm, they designed the restaurant with warm wooden interiors, soft lantern lighting, and delicate cherry blossom murals that echo their roots.

Wasabi offers two standout experiences: a bountiful buffet and a carefully curated sushi menu. The buffet, displayed on an elegant counter, is a rotating showcase of Japanese comfort dishes that reflect the seasons. From crispy karaage to fragrant bowls of udon, every dish is prepared with care, ensuring a perfect balance of tradition and taste. On the other hand, the sushi menu is Yukio's personal masterpiece. At the sushi counter, he crafts rolls and nigiri with an artist's precision, using the freshest seafood and rice he seasons to perfection. Guests often watch in quiet awe as he works, his movements quick and fluid, a testament to decades of dedication to his craft.

The restaurant is more than a place to eat—it is a labor of love and a community hub. Yukio and Hana personally greet every guest, sharing their passion for Japanese culture and cuisine with each interaction. Their story resonates with many: the leap of faith, the long nights perfecting recipes, and the joy of seeing their dream come alive. Whether diners are savoring a steaming bowl of miso soup from the buffet or indulging in the delicate flavors of a salmon nigiri, every moment at Wasabi feels like an intimate glimpse into the couple’s journey, connecting Helsinki to Kyoto with every bite.
